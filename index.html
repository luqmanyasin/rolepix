About us

Learn a little more about us, who we are and what we do

Learn more about us

Rolepix consists of a group of multi-talented design enthusiasts with years of extensive industrial experience & qualifications. We are fundamentally focused on brand strategy, digital design and web design & marketing.

Our primary and focal objective is to provide our clients with creative and practical design work, and visual communication services. We wish to develop a long-term win-win partnership with our clients.

If you’re searching for a group of proficient cutting-edge designers coordinated by experienced industry insiders… contact us now!

Web Design + Development

We design modern and imaginative website solutions from wireframe to functioning and grand designs, we apply a unique and creative flair into truly effective work from start to finish.

User Interface + Experience

Our team delivers uniquely innovative and elegant user-interface designs that incorporate decisive elements of information and user experience into a single seamless whole.

Illustration Design

Our expansive team of professional, award winning illustrators strive to bring your projects alive with the industry's best designers, targeting all markets around the world.

Visual Branding Identity Design

We define and enhance your brand with clarity and consistency across all touchpoints, with a brilliant mix of brand strategy, marketing strategy and creative work.
