What We Offer

Discover the various services we offer as a creative design agency

WEB DESIGN + DEVELOPMENT

Wireframe & Prototyping

These high-fidelity digital wireframes display the visual layout and flow of a web page, we create these before we proceed onto the actual design and development of the website. This process saves both parties a great deal of time, as it will prevent us from getting bogged down with the design details such as colour, typography or any other visual flairs further down the line.

Initial Design

This is the more visual and pretty side of your website. We offer fully layered website designs which are primarily designed in Photoshop. We offer to sell strictly PSD files, however we heavily recommend that you stick with us for the conversion and development of your website from zero to one to ensure you have a great website.

Our Photoshop designers have years of extensive experience in graphic production software applications, with the most well-known ones being Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat and Dreamweaver. We never stick to one software application, as our goal is to build you the perfect website for your business.

Front-end Development

Our front-end website designers have a robust understanding of the fundamentals of design principles, paired with valuable knowledge in the main languages such as HTML 5, CSS 3 and JavaScript, using their various libraries (such as jQuery, React, Angular and etc.) which are written in industry standard pre-processors.

We also excel in many other major factors when it comes to front-end development, such as designing websites optimised for different screen resolutions and devices, therefore every website we are responsible for creating is responsive to screen resolution and looks good on whichever device you’re browsing from. In addition to this, if you have a preference, we offer to work with any framework of your choice during the design stages such as Bootstrap.

The website designs that we create are also cross browser compatible, meaning that if you’re visiting with Internet Explorer or Firefox, the website will be completely functioning, regardless of which major browser you may be using.

We understand how your business revolves around marketing and advertising and how crucial it is, therefore our designers create your website with SEO in mind, ensuring that your content ranks well in the major search engines such as Google and Bing. This is what makes us different to others, we write good code with the future of your business in mind.

Back-end Development

If you want a more complex dynamic website, our back-end website developers are by far your greatest option. Our team take pride in building great, readable and well-documented code with an eye for QA.

Our diverse web-development team have strong skills and experience with Javascript (Node.js / Browser), Java, Python and PHP – along with this, we acquire knowledge in C, C++, Objective C and Swift. We also understand that your website will need a secure and reliable storage back-end system hence our team have knowledge in database systems such as MySQL, CouchDB and PostgreSQL, taking into account database constraints, locking, triggers and so on.

We build around Content Management and eCommerce systems such as Wordpress and Shopify, making content publishing and other daily tasks extremely easy, even for the non-technical minded.
